####################
Hygon DCU Chip for PaddlePaddle Operation
####################

DCU (Deep Computing Unit) is an accelerator of Hygon specialized in artificial intelligence and deep learning. The ROCm version of PaddlePaddle supports model training and inference on the CPU and DCU of Hygon.
